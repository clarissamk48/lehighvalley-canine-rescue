OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide a safe haven for all dogs in the Lehigh Valley.

LVCR strives to be the leading advocate for breeds such as pit bulls, who are often left behind or ignore by other rescue's. We strive to provide start of the art medical care for all animals and to offer financial assistance to those pet owners who cannot afford services at "for profit" veterinary clinics. LVCR will always try to match responsible pet seekers with their next best friend in order to find the perfect home for the animals in our care.

VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITIES

DOG WALKERS

Dog walkers provide much needed exercise to the dogs throughout the day, along with one on one socialization and companionshipfor our shelter dogs.

* All dog walkers must be over 18 or 16 and accompanied by a parent or guardian at all times.

SHELTER HELPERS

The shelter staff assists with laundry, dishes, basic cleaning, sorting donations and preparing enrichment treats for our shelter animals. Throughout the year we have other volunteer opportunities that are not listed above.

*Please feel free to inquire about seasonal volunteer opportunities we may have.
